WHAT IS SURP?

SURP (Summer Undergraduate Research Programme) as the name suggests is a centralised process for project allocation for research enthusiastic undergraduate students. This helps them to undertake projects in their own departments and also help in launching inter-departmental projects in their areas of interest. It encourages students to take up hands-on research experiences over their summer break and later into the semester under IIT Bombay professors and their teams during the semester.

Why SURP?

    A great opportunity to explore research as a career under the guidance of experts.

  • Explore multiple domains

    SURP brings you projects from different departments to quench your thirst of exploring multiple domains.

  • Explore interdisciplinary fields

    We have projects that let you explore your combined interest in multiple fields.

  • Work with professors

    Get a chance to work with experienced professors of the institute who have exemplary research records.

  • Get certified

    On successful completion of the projects, we provide you with a digital certificate signed by Dean RnD, IIT Bombay.

    Get a chance to present your work in a pan-India UG research conference - ResCon 2024 to be held at the start of next year.

Tenzies

This project is a digital version of a dice game. The objective is to keep rolling ten dice until all of them display the same number.
